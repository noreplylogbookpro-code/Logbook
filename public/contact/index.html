Frequently Asked Questions

Question: What is Logbook Plus?

Answer: Logbook Plus is a digital logbook for tracking entries with photos and financial details.

Question: Is my data secure?

Answer: Your data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption. All backups remain under your control with transparent storage policies.

Question: How do I export my data?

Answer: Select date range then select to export data in Excel (.xlsx) and Word (.docx)

Question: Can I attach receipts?

Answer: Yes, you can attach multiple receipts to each entry.

Question: Does it support backups?

Answer: Both automatic and manual backups are supported.

Question: Is dark mode available?

Answer: Yes, dark and light themes are available.

Question: Can I restore data?

Answer: You can restore data from encrypted backups.

Question: Is internet required?

Answer: No, the app works fully offline.

Question: How can I contact support?

Answer: Contact us at noreply.logbookpro@gmail.com

Question: Is the app free?

Answer: Yes, core features are free.

Question: Which formats are supported?

Answer: Excel (.xlsx) and Word (.docx).

Question: How do I add an entry?

Answer: Tap the + button and fill in the details.

Question: How do I export a backup?

Answer: Go to Export → Select date range → Export.

Question: How do I restore a backup?

Answer: Go to Settings → Backup → Import Backup.

Question: Where are photos stored?

Answer: Photos are stored in app-specific storage and included in backups.

Question: Can I delete an entry?

Answer: Yes, long-press an entry and select Delete.

Question: Can I add multiple entries?

Answer: No, entries can be added one at a time.

Question: Can I delete multiple entries?

Answer: Yes, long-press and select multiple entries to delete.

Question: Can I share my backup?

Answer: Yes, backups can be shared securely.

Question: Can I encrypt my backup?

Answer: Yes, backups can be encrypted.

Question: Can I change my backup password?

Answer: Yes, you can change your backup password anytime.

Question: How long does support take?

Answer: Most inquiries receive a response within 24-48 hours.

Question: Can I transfer backup to another device?

Answer: Yes, restore your latest backup on any supported device.

Question: Do you accept feature suggestions?

Answer: Absolutely, community feedback helps shape our roadmap.
