Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Subscription Refunds

Subscriptions purchased via our Android application are processed securely by Google Play. We offer a 7-day refund window from the date of your initial purchase. If you are not satisfied with the premium features within the first 7 days, you may request a full refund.

Refunds are subject to Google Play's refund policies. After the 7-day window, subscriptions are non-refundable and partial refunds for unused subscription periods will not be issued.

2. Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, you must:

  • Submit your request within 7 days of the original purchase date.
  • Provide proof of purchase (order ID or email confirmation).
  • Not have violated the Terms of Service.

Refunds will not be granted for accounts that have been suspended or terminated due to policy violations.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, you can use the Google Play Store's built-in refund flow (available in your Google Play account purchase history). Alternatively, you can contact us at noreply.logbookpro@gmail.com with your Google Play Transaction ID (GPA.XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXXX) and registered email. Eligible refund requests submitted within our 7-day window will be processed by us via the Google Play Console within 5–10 business days.

4. One-Time License Refunds

One-time license purchases (e.g., Self-Hosted License) are non-refundable once the license key has been issued or activated. Please carefully review the features before purchasing.

5. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be communicated through an app update or notification. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

6. Contact

For refund requests or questions about this policy, please use the Help section within the app.
