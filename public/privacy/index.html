Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 4, 2026

Thank you for using Logbook Plus. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

Logbook Plus does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data with the developer or with third parties. The application works entirely offline and does not require an account, login, or internet connection for its core functionality.

For users who choose to purchase premium features or subscriptions via the Android app, transaction processing is handled securely by Google Play. We do not collect, store, or have access to your credit card, bank account, or billing details. We only receive purchase verification tokens and transaction IDs from Google Play to unlock paid features on your device.

All information you enter in the app (such as expense logs, notes, and attached media) is stored only on your device or your personal cloud storage (if you choose to enable encrypted backups).

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores your data locally in its private storage area. This may include expense log entries, attached images, backup archives, and app settings. Your data is never uploaded to our servers or any external service unless you explicitly configure your own cloud destination (e.g., WebDAV, Nextcloud).

Example: /Android/data/com.logbookplus/files/

3. Backup and Restore

Logbook Plus provides optional backup and restore features so you can protect your data.

  • Backup files are created and stored locally or on your personal cloud server.
  • You decide when and where to export or share a backup file.
  • All backups are encrypted with AES-256 before leaving your device if you enable encryption.
  • No backup data is ever sent to Logbook Plus infrastructure.

4. Cloud Storage & Self-Hosting

If you configure a personal cloud server (WebDAV, SFTP, or Nextcloud), your encrypted backup files are transferred directly to your own server. Logbook Plus does not have access to your server credentials or the content of your backups. You retain full ownership and control.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect your data within the app. Data is stored in your device's private app storage area. However, the security of your device (screen lock, OS updates, etc.) is your responsibility. For cloud backups, encryption ensures that even if your cloud storage is compromised, your data remains unreadable without your password.

6. Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over your data. You can view, edit, or delete entries at any time. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data within the app's private storage will be permanently deleted unless you have exported a backup beforehand.

7. Permissions

Permissions (like Storage or Notifications) are used only for functional purposes — saving backups, showing operation status, or accessing local files. The app does not use permissions for advertising, tracking, or analytics.

8. No Ads, No Tracking

Logbook Plus does not include advertising, analytics libraries, third-party tracking, or any form of telemetry. Your usage patterns remain completely private.

9. Children's Privacy

Logbook Plus does not collect personal information and is not directed toward children. We do not knowingly process data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Us
