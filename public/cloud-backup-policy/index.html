Cloud Backup Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Cloud Backup Availability

Cloud backup is a premium feature available exclusively to users with an active premium subscription. Free users can use local backup (device storage) at no cost and without restrictions.

2. Storage Limit

Premium users are allocated up to 240 MB of cloud storage for backup files. This limit applies to the total size of all backup files stored in the cloud for your account.

If you reach the 240 MB limit, you must delete older backups before uploading new ones. The app will notify you when you are approaching or have reached the storage limit.

3. Data Retention

Your cloud backups are retained for as long as your premium subscription remains active. Upon expiry or cancellation of your subscription:

  • You will have a 30-day grace period to download your backups.
  • After the 30-day grace period, cloud backup data may be permanently deleted.

We strongly recommend downloading a local copy of your data before your subscription expires.

4. Data Security

All cloud backup files are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. We do not access the content of your backup files. You may optionally enable AES-256 password encryption for your backups for additional security.

5. Data Ownership

You retain full ownership of your backup data. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party. Your data is used solely to provide the cloud backup service.

6. Liability

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, we cannot guarantee against data loss due to server failures, natural disasters, or other events beyond our control. We strongly encourage maintaining local backups in addition to cloud backups.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cloud Backup Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via the app. Your continued use of cloud backup after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
